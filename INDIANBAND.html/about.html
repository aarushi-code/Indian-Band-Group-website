INDE ROCKERS

ABOUT

The Inde Rockers is an Indian Hindi Rock band originally from Mumbai, consisting of Ram Mehra on lead vocals and guitar, Yohan Roy on backing vocals and bass guitar, Abhay Negi on lead guitar and backing vocals and Aakarsh Dev on drums and percussions. Known for singles like Vaqif, Nazrein, Zid,Bheed me, The Inde Rockers is one of the biggest contemporary Indie bands of the country. Its track Ye Kaisi was featured in the award-winning film Angry Indian Goddesses.

The band has performed in ace music festivals like NH7 Weekenders and VH1 Supersonic and will be next performing as one of the opening acts of OnePlus Music Festival featuring Dua Lipa and Katy Perry.
